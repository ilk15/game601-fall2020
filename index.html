(Midterm) Non-Digital Game: Spoonies
Jokingly called "Mental Illness: The Card Game" during development. Spoons are part of an allegory used to explain the limited engery assiciated with some chronic health conditions. [LINK]. Some people who have these conditions use "spoons" as slang, ex: "I don't have the spoons to do that"/"I'm out of spoons", and some refer to themselves as Spoonies. This is a game about living as a Spoonie and trying to keep up with daily life.
